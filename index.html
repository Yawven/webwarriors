ABOUT US




Welcome to Web Warriors, a group of hardworking, and skilled students from BSIE-ICT 3A consisting of Katlyn Evangelista, Kayrolyne Castañares, Mary Perino, JC Roda, Dominic San Juan, and lead by Joven Velo. Our group is dedicated in creating beautiful and useful program-based projects, and we are looking forward into expanding our field of expertise.


Based in the prestigious Technological University of the Philippines - Cavite, Web Warriors is supported by the best professors in the field of Information Technology. And with their mentorship and our hard-work, no program-based project is safe from us. We are the Web Warriors.
